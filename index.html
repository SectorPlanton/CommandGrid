🎖 FIELD MANUAL — COMMAND GRID

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Destroy the enemy HQ or complete mission objectives. Manage command points, supply, and morale — a demoralized army fights at reduced effectiveness.

🔄 TURN STRUCTURE

  • Deploy Phase — Spend Supply to place units in your base zone
  • Battle Phase — Move and attack with units (2 AP each)
  • Press COMMENCE BATTLE to end deploy, END TURN to pass

⚡ RESOURCES

  • Supply 🎒 — Spent deploying. Gain +3 each round
  • Command ⚡ — Powers abilities. Gain +2/round (max 10)
  • Morale 🛡 — -10% per unit lost. Low morale = less attack
  • Objectives ⭐ — Yields +1 Supply/turn when controlled

🗺 TERRAIN

  • 🌲 Forest — +1 defense, cover
  • ⛰ Mountain — +2 defense, blocks ground movement
  • 🌊 Water — Impassable (Drone flies over)
  • ⭐ Objective — Capture by moving onto it
  • 🔷/🔶 Base — Your deployment zone

🪖 UNIT ROSTER

  • 🪖 Infantry (3) — Balanced frontliner
  • 🎯 Sniper (5) — Range 4, high damage, fragile
  • 🔭 Recon (4) — Fast, wide vision
  • 🚁 Drone (6) — Flies water, area damage
  • 🛡 Tank (8) — Max armor + siege
  • 💊 Medic (4) — Heals adjacent allies
  • 🏛 HQ — Protect at all costs!

👁 FOG OF WAR

Only cells within your units' vision are visible. Enemy units in fog are hidden. Deploy Recon to extend sight lines. Snipers have exceptional vision (5 cells).

✨ SPECIAL ABILITIES

  • Sniper — Overwatch: Intercepts enemy moves in range
  • Medic — Field Surgery: +30 HP to adjacent ally (2 CMD)
  • Drone — Airstrike: Hits all in 3x3 area (3 CMD)
  • Recon — Scout: Reveals area (1 CMD)
  • Infantry — Fortify: +5 defense bonus
  • Tank — Siege Fire: Massive strike (2 CMD)

📱 CONTROLS

  • Select unit type → click base cell to deploy
  • Battle Phase: click your unit → green = move range
  • Switch to FIRE ORDER → red = attack range
  • SPECIAL OPS → use abilities on valid targets
  • Landscape orientation required on mobile
